If you’ve been working with GoIP gateways, you know one uncomfortable fact: the better your termination business goes — the more aggressively you’re hunted. Carriers improve anti-fraud. Regulators in dozens of countries view termination as a threat. Équipement gets blocked. SIMs burn out.

This doesn’t mean your knowledge and equipment are useless. It means there’s a way to use them differently.

Pourquoi la terminaison Attire l'attention

Anti-fraude opérateur

Sortant traffic from a GoIP gateway is instantly visible. Too many calls from one number, unusual timing, atypical geography — these are red flags. SIMs get blocked.

Pression réglementaire

In some countries, termination is classified as a telecom law violation. Russia, Iran, China and others actively prosecute operators. This is a real legal risk.

Instabilité des revenus

A wave of bans can destroy a week’s or month’s profit in a single day. Hard to plan, hard to scale.

What GoIPPro Offers

GoIPPro works with the same equipment — same gateways, same SIM cards. But in a fundamentally different mode: inbound traffic only.

We don’t land calls. We don’t generate outbound traffic. Our system receives SMS and inbound calls — a completely different activity profile. To the carrier, your SIM looks like a regular subscriber who occasionally receives messages. No red flags. SIMs last for months.
