Проблема, которую вы знаете слишком хорошо

You pay an SMS aggregator to deliver 1 million messages a month. They report "97% delivered." Looks great on paper. Here's what actually happens:

👻

Фальшивые отчёты о доставке

Your provider reпортов "delivered" — but the message never reached a real phone. The DLR was generated by an intermediate hub, not by the destination network. You're paying for air.

✏️

Подмена контента

Your OTP says "Your code is 4821." What the customer receives: "Your code is 4821. Download our app: [spam link]." Grey routes inject ads into your messages.

🏷️

Изменение Sender ID

Вы отправляете от «YourBrand». Клиент видит случайный номер или другое имя. Ваш бренд выглядит как спам. Доверие испаряется.

🔇

Тихие сбои

Messages to certain operators or countries simply vanish. No error, no DLR failure — just silence. Your customers can't log in, can't verify, can't transact.

Почему мы отличаемся

The SMS testing market has established players — TelQ, Testelium, TestMySMS. Here's why GoIPPro Верификация is a fundamentally different proposition.

Live subscribers, not test numbers

Other platforms maintain dedicated test number pools. Smart providers learn to recognize and whitelist them. Our SIMs are part of a live B2B traffic network — they receive real calls and SMS every day. There is nothing to whitelist because there is nothing unusual about them.

Coverage where it matters

Most testing platforms excel in Western Europe and the US. Try testing delivery to MTN Нигерия, Иранcell Иран, or Jazz Пакистан. GoIPPro has local SIMs actively working in these exact markets. We don't stretch to cover them — we live there.

Anti-whitelisting by design

Наши SIM несут реальный ежедневный трафик и ротируются естественно. SIM, которая тестирует вашу доставку сегодня, вчера приняла 15 реальных звонков. Ни один провайдер не может отличить тестовый номер от обычного.

Lower cost. Infrastructure already exists.

Testing platforms build SIM networks specifically for testing — expensive. Our SIM network already exists and is funded by our core traffic business. Verification is an incremental service on top of existing infrastructure. Better prices, same quality.
