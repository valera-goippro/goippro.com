The Problem You Know Too Well

You pay an SMS aggregator to deliver 1 million messages a month. They report "97% delivered." Looks great on paper. Here's what actually happens:

👻

Fake Delivery Reports

Your provider reports "delivered" — but the message never reached a real phone. The DLR was generated by an intermediate hub, not by the destination network. You're paying for air.

✏️

Content Manipulation

Your OTP says "Your code is 4821." What the customer receives: "Your code is 4821. Download our app: [spam link]." Grey routes inject ads into your messages.

🏷️

Sender ID Changes

You send from "YourBrand." The customer sees a random number or a different sender name. Your brand looks like spam. Trust evaporates.

🔇

Silent Failures

Messages to certain operators or countries simply vanish. No error, no DLR failure — just silence. Your customers can't log in, can't verify, can't transact.

You can't fix what you can't see. That's where GoIPPro Verificar comes in.

Why We're Different

The SMS testing market has established players — TelQ, Testelium, TestMySMS. Here's why GoIPPro Verificar is a fundamentally different proposition.

Live subscribers, not test numbers

Other platforms maintain dedicated test number pools. Smart providers learn to recognize and whitelist them. Our SIMs are part of a live B2B traffic network — they receive real calls and SMS every day. There is nothing to whitelist because there is nothing unusual about them.

Coverage where it matters

Most testing platforms excel in Western Europe and the US. Try testing delivery to MTN Nigeria, Irancell Iran, or Jazz Pakistan. GoIPPro has local SIMs actively working in these exact markets. We don't stretch to cover them — we live there.

Anti-whitelisting by design

Our SIMs carry real daily traffic and rotate naturally. We don't need to "refresh" test numbers artificially. The SIM that tests your delivery today received 15 real calls yesterday. No provider can whitelist what they can't distinguish from regular traffic.

Lower cost. Infrastructure already exists.

Testing platforms build SIM networks specifically for testing — expensive. Our SIM network already exists and is funded by our core traffic business. Verification is an incremental service on top of existing infrastructure. Better prices, same quality.
