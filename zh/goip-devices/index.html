GoIP Gateways —
选择您的设备

A GoIP gateway accepts SIM cards and processes SMS and voice traffic. This is what earns money — not a phone, not a server.

什么是GoIP网关？ An industrial device with SIM card slots, GSM modules, and a network interface. Connects to the internet, registers on the mobile network, and passes real mobile traffic through it. Uses 5–25W — like a light bulb. Runs 24/7 without a computer.
