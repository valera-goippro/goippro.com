GoIP Gateways —
Choose Your Device

We help you choose the right GoIP gateway and use it most efficiently. Not a shop — a platform that turns your device into passive income.

What Is a GoIP Gateway? An industrial device with SIM card slots, GSM modules, and a network interface. Connects to the internet, registers on the mobile network, and passes real mobile traffic through it. Uses 5–25W — like a light bulb. Runs 24/7 without a computer.
We don’t sell devices. GoIPPro is a platform that helps you use GoIP gateways most efficiently. Below we show which models work best and what income to expect. Need help finding a device? Contact our support — we’ll connect you with trusted sellers.

Good to Know

Power Consumption

5–25W depending on model. Electricity costs $5–20 per year.

Internet

Stable wired or WiFi connection. Usage: 1–5 GB per month per port for voice, less for SMS.

Management

Gateway is configured via web interface. Phone is only needed for dashboard and Telegram notifications.
