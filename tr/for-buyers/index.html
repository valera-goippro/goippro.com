Frequently Asked Questions

How is your traffic different from other GSM providers?

We operate a managed network — not a marketplace. Every micro-provider is vetted, every channel is monitored 24/7 by AI and human operators. canales behave like real subscribers because we control load distribution, rotation cycles, and USSD health checks. The result: consistent quality you can rely on for production routing.

What happens if a channel gets flagged or banned?

Our monitoring system detects anomalies before operators do. Suspicious canales are automatically isolated from your traffic within seconds. The provider is notified, the channel is replaced, and your routes continue uninterrupted. You never see degraded quality.

Can I test before committing?

Absolutely. We provide a test trunk with real traffic in your target markets. Taahhüt yok, no minimum volume. You evaluate ASR, ACD, and CLI quality firsthand before scaling up.

What countries can you scale quickly?

We operate in 30+ countries across Asia, Africa, Middle East, Europe, and Latin America. We can launch a new country within 2–4 weeks if you need specific coverage.

Do you handle outbound termination?

No. GoIPPro is inbound only — incoming SMS and incoming voice calls. This is a deliberate decision: inbound-only means zero risk of channel bans from operators, no spam complaints, and dramatically longer channel lifetimes. It's why our channel'ler aylarca dayanır instead of days.

What are your pricing models?

We offer per-minute (voice) and per-message (SMS) pricing, with volume tiers. Rates vary by country and capacity commitment. Contact us for a custom quote based on your target markets and expected volumes.
