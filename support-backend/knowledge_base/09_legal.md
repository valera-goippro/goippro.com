# Legal & Compliance

## Is This Legal?
- GoIPPro operates an **inbound-only** traffic model
- Your device RECEIVES calls — it does NOT initiate outgoing calls
- This model is legal in most jurisdictions worldwide
- We recommend consulting local telecom regulations in your specific country
- Partners across 25+ countries actively use the platform

## Inbound vs Outbound (Origination vs Termination)
- **Termination (outbound)**: Receiving VoIP calls and sending them out as GSM calls. This IS often illegal and causes SIM blocks.
- **Origination (inbound)**: Receiving incoming GSM calls/SMS and routing them to VoIP buyers. This is the standard telecom business model.
- GoIPPro does ONLY origination — we are NOT a termination platform
