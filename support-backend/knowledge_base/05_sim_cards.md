# SIM Cards

## Requirements
- Active SIM cards from local mobile operators in your country
- Prepaid or postpaid — both work
- Data plan NOT required (traffic goes through internet, not SIM data)
- Voice and SMS capability enabled on the SIM

## SIM Card Tips
- Use SIM cards from major operators in your country for best traffic
- Some operators have better rates for inbound calls — check locally
- Keep SIM cards topped up / active (don't let them expire)
- If a SIM gets blocked or deactivated, replace it and notify support

## Why SIMs Don't Get Blocked
- GoIPPro uses **inbound-only** traffic model
- Your device RECEIVES calls and SMS — never initiates outgoing traffic
- Mobile operators block SIMs that make suspicious outgoing calls (termination)
- Since we only do origination (inbound), operators have no reason to block
- This is the key difference from traditional GSM termination

## SIM Management
- You manage your own SIM cards (purchase, top-up, replacement)
- Dashboard shows which SIM slots are active/inactive
- If a SIM stops working, swap it for a new one
- No special SIM registration required beyond standard local rules

## How Many SIMs Do I Need?
- One SIM per GoIP port
- GoIP 4 = 4 SIMs
- GoIP 8 = 8 SIMs
- GoIP 16 = 16 SIMs
- GoIP 32 = 32 SIMs
- Keep 2-3 spare SIMs in case you need to replace one quickly
