# Earnings & Payments

## How Much Can I Earn?

### Per SIM Card (monthly estimates)
- SMS-only traffic: $5–15 per SIM
- Voice + SMS combined: $8–25 per SIM
- Verify program (SMS route testing): $12–35 per SIM

### Per Device (monthly estimates)
- GoIP 4 (4 ports): $30–100/month
- GoIP 8 (8 ports): $80–200/month
- GoIP 16 (16 ports): $150–400/month
- GoIP 32 (32 ports): $250–800+/month

### ROI Calculator
- Formula: Number of ports × Rate per port = Monthly income
- Example: 8 ports × $7/port = $56/month
- GoIP 8 costs ~$200, payback in ~3.5 months at $7/port
- GoIP 32 costs ~$350, payback in ~2.5 months at 32×$5=$160/month

### Rates Vary By
- Country/market demand
- Traffic type (voice, SMS, verify)
- Channel quality (MOS score, uptime)
- Volume of ports connected
