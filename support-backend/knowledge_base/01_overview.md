# GoIPPro — Platform Overview

## What is GoIPPro?
GoIPPro is a professional B2B platform for GoIP channel rental. Partners connect their GoIP gateway devices to our platform and earn monthly passive income in USDT/USDC cryptocurrency.

## Business Model
- **Origination only** — we handle INBOUND traffic. Partner devices RECEIVE calls and SMS, never initiate them.
- This model protects SIM cards from operator blocks (unlike termination where SIMs get blocked quickly).
- Traffic comes from wholesale B2B buyers — telecom operators and aggregators.
- GoIPPro manages all routing, quality monitoring, and client relationships.

## Key Value Propositions
- **No SIM blocks**: Inbound-only traffic means operators don't detect violations. SIM cards stay alive longer.
- **Voice + SMS**: Both traffic types on one gateway. No other B2B platform offers this combination.
- **Crypto payments**: USDT/USDC — no bank transfers needed. Works in sanctioned countries.
- **Enterprise hardware**: GoIP gateways only, not smartphones or personal SIMs. 99.5% uptime SLA.
- **Global coverage**: Partners in 25+ countries already earning.

## Platform Stats
- 99.5% Uptime SLA
- 10-day channel validation period
- USDT / USDC payments (TRC-20 or ERC-20)
- API available for resellers
- Dashboard for monitoring earnings and channel status

## Who Is This For?
1. **Professional (PRO)**: Already works with GoIP/GSM termination, wants stable inbound income without SIM blocks.
2. **Beginner (NEW)**: Wants passive income, needs step-by-step guidance and 24/7 support.
3. **Zero-touch (ZT)**: Non-technical user — plug in device, it auto-connects and starts earning. (Coming soon)
