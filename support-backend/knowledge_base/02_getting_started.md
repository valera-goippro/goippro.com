# Getting Started with GoIPPro

## 5 Steps to Start Earning

### Step 1: Register
- Create an account on GoIPPro via Email or Telegram.
- Takes about 2 minutes.
- No documents required for basic onboarding.

### Step 2: Submit Channel Request
- Specify your country, GoIP device model, and number of ports.
- We match you with the right B2B buyer pool for your region.
- Some countries have higher demand — ask support which markets are hot right now.

### Step 3: 10-Day Validation
- Automated quality check begins:
  - Voice quality (MOS score)
  - SMS delivery rate
  - Connection stability
- Channels that pass validation earn a higher rate per port.
- You don't need to do anything — the process is fully automated.
- During validation, you may receive test traffic.

### Step 4: Channel Goes Live
- Your channel is activated in the B2B pool.
- Starts receiving traffic from wholesale buyers.
- Monitoring dashboard shows real-time status.
- You can see earnings per SIM, uptime, and traffic stats.

### Step 5: Receive USDT / USDC
- Monthly payouts to your crypto wallet.
- Automatic — no manual requests needed.
- Minimum payout: $10.
- Choose TRC-20 or ERC-20 network.
- Works in sanctioned countries.

## What You Need
1. A GoIP gateway device (GoIP 4, 8, 16, or 32 ports)
2. Active SIM cards for your country
3. Stable internet connection (1 Mbps per 8 ports, wired preferred)
4. Power supply
5. A USDT/USDC crypto wallet for payments
