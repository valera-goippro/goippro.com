# Frequently Asked Questions — Quick Reference

## Q: How much can I realistically earn per SIM?
A: SMS-only: $5–15/SIM/month. Voice+SMS: $8–25/SIM. Verify program: $12–35/SIM. GoIP-8 typically earns $80–200/month. GoIP-32 fleet: $250–800+/month.

## Q: Do I need to know how my SIM cards are used?
A: No. We handle all traffic routing, quality monitoring, and client management. Your job: keep device online and SIMs active. Our job: fill channels with premium traffic and pay you.

## Q: What GoIP devices are supported?
A: All GoIP hardware gateways: GoIP 4, 8, 16, 32, UC2000-WG, and compatible models. No smartphones or personal SIM farms.

## Q: What internet speed do I need?
A: 1 Mbps per 8 ports. GoIP 32 needs 4 Mbps stable upload. Wired connection strongly preferred over WiFi.

## Q: How and when are payments made?
A: Monthly in USDT or USDC (TRC-20 or ERC-20). Minimum $10. Automatic to your wallet. No manual requests.

## Q: Is this legal in my country?
A: Inbound-only traffic is legal in most jurisdictions. We recommend checking local telecom regulations. Partners in 25+ countries use the platform.

## Q: What happens during the 10-day validation?
A: Automated testing of voice quality (MOS), SMS delivery rate, and connection stability. Higher quality = higher rate. Fully automated, you don't need to do anything.

## Q: Do I need technical knowledge?
A: Basic setup follows our step-by-step guide (6 languages). Zero-touch devices (coming soon) need only power and internet.

## Q: Can I connect from any country?
A: We operate in 25+ countries. Contact support to check if your country has active demand.

## Q: What if my device goes offline?
A: Your channel temporarily leaves the active pool. Bring it back online and it re-enters automatically. Extended downtime may require re-validation.

## Q: Can I scale up with more devices?
A: Absolutely. Many partners run multiple GoIP devices. More ports = more income. Volume discounts may apply.

## Q: Do I need a crypto wallet?
A: Yes. You need a USDT/USDC wallet (TRC-20 or ERC-20). We recommend Trust Wallet, MetaMask, or any compatible wallet.
