# GoIP Equipment & Hardware

## Supported Devices
- **GoIP 4** — 4 SIM ports, compact, good for beginners. ~$120-150
- **GoIP 8** — 8 SIM ports, most popular model. ~$200
- **GoIP 16** — 16 SIM ports, mid-range fleet. ~$280
- **GoIP 32** — 32 SIM ports, maximum density. ~$350
- **UC2000-WG** — enterprise-grade, compatible models
- We do NOT support smartphones or personal SIM farms — enterprise hardware only.

## What You Need
- GoIP gateway device (any model above)
- Active SIM cards for your country's mobile operators
- Stable internet connection: minimum 1 Mbps per 8 ports
  - GoIP 8: 1 Mbps stable
  - GoIP 16: 2 Mbps stable
  - GoIP 32: 4 Mbps stable upload recommended
- Wired ethernet connection strongly preferred over WiFi for 99.5% uptime
- Power supply (included with device)

## Where to Buy GoIP Devices
- AliExpress — search "GoIP 8 gateway" or "GoIP 32 gateway"
- Alibaba — for bulk orders
- Local telecom equipment suppliers
- Approximate payback period: ~5 months for GoIP 8 at $7/port rate

## Device Setup Overview
1. Insert SIM cards into the GoIP device
2. Connect ethernet cable to your router
3. Connect power supply
4. Access GoIP web interface (default: 192.168.8.1)
5. Configure SIP settings to point to GoIPPro server
6. Our platform auto-detects and validates channels
