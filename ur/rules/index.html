About the Legal Side

Each country has its own legislation on commercial SIM use. GoIPPro operates as a technology platform — we provide infrastructure, not a telecom license. Understanding local regulations is the partner's responsibility.

A key difference in our model: we work with inbound traffic only. No outbound call termination. This fundamentally changes the legal profile compared to traditional GoIP schemes.
