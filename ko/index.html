자주 묻는 질문

Quick answers for partners worldwide

SIM당 실제로 얼마나 벌 수 있나요? +

It depends on traffic type. SMS-only traffic: $5–15 per SIM 월. 음성 + SMS combined: $8–25 per SIM. Partners participating in our 인증 program (SMS route testing) can earn $12–35 per SIM. Most partners with a GoIP-8 earn $80–200/month. A GoIP-32 fleet: $250–800+/month.

SIM 카드가 어떻게 사용되는지 알아야 하나요? +

No. We handle all traffic routing, quality monitoring, and client management. Your job: keep the device online and SIM cards active. Our job: fill your channels with premium traffic and pay you every month. You see earnings per SIM in your dashboard — that’s all you need.

어떤 GoIP 장치가 지원되나요? +

We support all GOIP hardware gateways: GoIP 4, GoIP 8, GoIP 16, GoIP 32, UC2000-WG, and compatible models. We do not use smartphones or personal SIM farms — enterprise hardware only.

어떤 인터넷 속도가 필요한가요? +

A stable connection of 1 Mbps per 8 포트 is sufficient. For a GoIP 32 (32 ports), a 4 Mbps stable upload is recommended. A wired connection is strongly preferred over WiFi for 99.5% uptime.

결제는 어떻게, 언제 이루어지나요? +

Payments are made monthly in USDT or USDC (TRC-20 or ERC-20 — your choice). Minimum payout is $10. Automatic transfer to your specified wallet address. No manual requests needed.

제 나라에서 합법인가요? +

We operate inbound-only traffic — your device receives calls, not initiates them. This model is legal in most jurisdictions. We recommend consulting local telecom regulations. Partners in 30+ countries actively use the platform.

10일 검증 기간 동안 무슨 일이 일어나나요? +

Your channel is tested for voice quality (MOS score), SMS delivery rate, and connection stability. Channels that pass validation earn a higher rate per port. You don't need to do anything — the process is fully automated.

설정에 기술적 지식이 필요한가요? +

Basic setup requires following our step-by-step guide (available in 6 languages). If you want true plug-and-play, our 무설정 device (launching soon) requires only power and internet — no configuration whatsoever.
