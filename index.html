Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for new and existing partners

How much can I realistically earn per channel? +

It depends on traffic type. SMS-only traffic: $5–15 per channel per month. Voice + SMS combined: $8–25 per channel. Partners participating in our Verify program (route testing) can earn $12–35 per channel. Most partners with an 8-channel device earn $80–200/month. A 32-channel setup: $250–800+/month.

Do I need to know how my channels are used? +

No. We handle all traffic routing, quality monitoring, and client management. Your job: keep the device online and channels active. Our job: fill your channels with premium traffic and pay you every month. You see earnings per channel in your dashboard — that’s all you need.

What GoIP devices are supported? +

We support all GOIP hardware gateways: GoIP 4, GoIP 8, GoIP 16, GoIP 32, UC2000-WG, and compatible models. We use enterprise-grade gateway hardware only — no consumer devices.

What internet speed do I need? +

A stable connection of 1 Mbps per 8 ports is sufficient. For a GoIP 32 (32 ports), a 4 Mbps stable upload is recommended. A wired connection is strongly preferred over WiFi for 99.5% uptime.

How and when are payments made? +

Payments are made monthly using convenient payment methods chosen by the partner. Minimum payout is $10. Automatic transfer to your specified account. No manual requests needed.

Is this legal in my country? +

We operate inbound-only traffic — your device receives calls, not initiates them. This model is legal in most jurisdictions. We recommend consulting local telecom regulations. Partners across many countries actively use the platform. Contact support to check availability in your region.

What happens during the 10-day validation period? +

Your channel is tested for voice quality (MOS score), SMS delivery rate, and connection stability. Channels that pass validation earn a higher rate per port. You don't need to do anything — the process is fully automated.

Do I need technical knowledge to set up? +

Basic setup requires following our step-by-step guide (available in 6 languages). If you want true plug-and-play, our Zero-touch device (launching soon) requires only power and internet — no configuration whatsoever.
