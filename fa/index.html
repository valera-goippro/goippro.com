سؤالات متداول

پاسخ‌های سریع برای شرکای ترکیه، نیجریه، پاکستان، ایران

واقعاً از هر SIM چقدر می‌توانم درآمد داشته باشم؟ +

It depends on traffic type. SMS-only traffic: $5–15 per SIM ماهانه. صوت + پیامک combined: $8–25 per SIM. Partners participating in our تأیید program (SMS route testing) can earn $12–35 per SIM. Most partners with a GoIP-8 earn $80–200/month. A GoIP-32 fleet: $250–800+/month.

آیا باید بدانم سیم‌کارت‌هایم چگونه استفاده می‌شوند؟ +

No. We handle all traffic routing, quality monitoring, and client management. Your job: keep the device online and SIM cards active. Our job: fill your channels with premium traffic and pay you every month. You see earnings per SIM in your dashboard — that’s all you need.

کدام دستگاه‌های GoIP پشتیبانی می‌شوند؟ +

We support all GOIP hardware gateways: GoIP 4, GoIP 8, GoIP 16, GoIP 32, UC2000-WG, and compatible models. We do not use smartphones or personal SIM farms — enterprise hardware only.

چه سرعت اینترنتی لازم است؟ +

A stable connection of 1 Mbps per 8 پورت is sufficient. For a GoIP 32 (32 ports), a 4 Mbps stable upload is recommended. A wired connection is strongly preferred over WiFi for 99.5% uptime.

پرداخت‌ها چگونه و چه زمانی انجام می‌شود؟ +

Payments are made monthly in USDT or USDC (TRC-20 or ERC-20 — your choice). Minimum payout is $10. Automatic transfer to your specified wallet address. No manual requests needed.

آیا این در کشور من قانونی است؟ +

We operate inbound-only traffic — your device receives calls, not initiates them. This model is legal in most jurisdictions. We recommend consulting local telecom regulations. Partners in ترکیه, نیجریه, پاکستان, and ایران actively use the platform.

در دوره اعتبارسنجی 10 روزه چه اتفاقی می‌افتد؟ +

Your channel is tested for voice quality (MOS score), SMS delivery rate, and connection stability. Channels that pass validation earn a higher rate per port. You don't need to do anything — the process is fully automated.

آیا برای راه‌اندازی به دانش فنی نیاز دارم؟ +

Basic setup requires following our step-by-step guide (available in 6 languages). If you want true plug-and-play, our بدون تنظیم device (launching soon) requires only power and internet — no configuration whatsoever.
